We will extract the following features from the raw data (Tweets plus meta data):

- Emotional connotation (positive or negative)
- Word length
- Emotionality
- Number (proportion) of negatively connotated words
- Number (proportion) of positively connotated words
- most frequent words and bigrams
- Number (proporting) of exlamation marks relative to number of tweets
- Number (proportion) of exlamation marks relative to number of tweets
- Ratio of adjectives to adverbs


Out of this indicators, we will build a "populism measure".
